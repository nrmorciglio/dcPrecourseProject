About Me
Nicholas Morciglio

Nicholas Morciglio is 29 years old and he grew up in Catskill, NY. He also goes by Nick for short.

Nick comes from a background in event managament.
He worked for the sport headphone company, AfterShokz, and the nonprofit, Together We Rise

After realizing that event management was not a career that he wanted long term, he did extensive research on coding and web development bootcamps in order to learn if a career in tech would be right for him.

Nick is looking to start a new career for the freedom of working remote, learning new specialized skills, and to begina a lucrative career in tech.
That is when he came across DigitalCrafts.
